# RFC-009 — Public positioning and claim discipline

## Decision
All public-facing language must stay narrower than the architecture’s maximum ambition.

## Approved style
- “trajectory judge”
- “world-model-derived verifier signal”
- “early failure detection / trajectory ranking”

## Disallowed style
- “universal reward model”
- “general judge for RL”
- “solves RL reward shaping”

## Consequence
The benchmark claim always outranks the hype claim.
