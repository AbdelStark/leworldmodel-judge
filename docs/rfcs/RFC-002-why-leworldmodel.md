# RFC-002 — Why LeWorldModel

## Decision
Use **LeWorldModel** as the strategic conceptual anchor.

## Why
LeWorldModel best matches the intended story:
- JEPA-native
- world-model-first
- surprise / physical regularity framing
- fresh research taste for AMI-facing positioning

## Important caveat
This does not force a full faithful LeWorldModel reproduction in v1.

## Consequence
The README and research docs should name LeWorldModel explicitly, while the judge interface remains implementation-agnostic.
